Why Ursua Designs?

Ursua Designs employs the evolving technology of 3D printing to create unique items that hold a special value for anyone.

As it's expressed, a personality reveals the inner being of everyone. It shows us who we are and why we act as we do. As such, putting a personal touch on the things around us is an extension of our personalities and who we are.


Ursua Designs Capabilities

Ursua Designs owns two 3D printers, a heavily modifed Creality Ender 3, as well as a slightly modified TronXY X1. Both printers produce excellent prints and are used to accelerate printing and achieve different results. The largest print we support is the size of 8.5 inches x 8.5 inches x 9.5 inches.

Our prices are determine by a number of factors, including the size of the print, the material used, the level of detail required, as well as how much material is used.

Our Materials and Colors
  • PLA (our standard plastic): Black, White, Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow.
  • TPU (stretchy and flexible): Black, White, and Green.
  • PETG (more durable and resistant): Gray and Orange.
