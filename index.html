Why Ursua Designs?

Ursua Designs employs the evolving technology of 3D printing to create unique items that hold a special value for anyone.

As it's expressed, a personality reveals the inner being of everyone. It shows us who we are and why we act as we do. As such, putting a personal touch on the things around us is an extension of our personalities and who we are.


Ursua Designs Capabilities

Ursua Designs owns two 3D printers, a heavily modifed Creality Ender 3, as well as a slightly modified TronXY X1. Both printers produce excellent prints and are used to accelerate printing and achieve different results. The largest print we support is the size of 8.5 inches x 8.5 inches x 9.5 inches.

Our prices are determine by a number of factors, including the size of the print, the material used, the level of detail required, as well as how much material is used.

Our Materials and Colors
  • PLA (our standard plastic): Black, White, Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow.
  • TPU (stretchy and flexible): Black, White, and Green.
  • PETG (more durable and resistant): Gray and Orange.

Collapsing Daggers

Engaging and Shareable

$11.99

Blade Color

Hilt Color

Ursua Designs create fully customizable 3D printed items, like this toy collapsing dagger / knife!

Simply swing around for the blade within to go shooting out and form the full dagger! Then to put it away, just push the blade from the very top towards to hilt to collapse the blade it into itself.

All 3D printed components can have their color personalized to one of eight colors: Black, White, Gray, Red, Green, Blue, Yellow, and Orange. If you have any unique color requests, please be sure to email us.

The handle is made with jute string wrapped around the plastic of the hilt, giving an added layer of craftsmanship in addition to the added comfort in the hand.

This item is made of plastic so please be careful not to break it!

Note: Due to the nature of 3D printing, visible lines, marks, and other imperfections may be present in orders. Ursua Designs is not responsible for any type of damage with this mount, whether the damage was caused in a crash or not.
