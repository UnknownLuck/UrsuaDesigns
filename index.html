Why Ursua Designs?


Ursua Designs employs the evolving technology of 3D printing to create unique items that hold a special value for anyone.

We go out of our way to ensure we deliver the best possible result. 3D printing is an art that takes time and effort to produce a result worthy of being called final. For any given blemish or failure, we assess the situation and determine the best way to fix it. Should the need arise, Ursua Designs will reprint an object that doesn't meet our quality standards.

Everyone has their own unique personality. That's why it's important to express oneself as it is the most powerful way to show others who they are and why they do the things they do. As such, adding a personal bit of flair and personalization provides a special value that stands out.


Ursua Designs Capabilities

Ursua Designs has three 3D printers, two of which are heavily modifed Creality Ender 3s, alongside a slightly modified TronXY X1. All three printers produce excellent prints and are used to accelerate printing and achieve different results. The largest print we support is the size of 15 inches x 14 inches x 9 inches.

Our prices are determine by a number of factors, including the size of the print, the material used, the level of detail required, as well as how much material is used.

Our Materials and Colors
  • PLA (our standard plastic): Black, White, Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow.
  • TPU (stretchy and flexible): Black, White, and Green.
  • PETG (more durable and resistant): Gray and Orange.

VR Headset Stand and Controller Wall Mount

Display Your Prized VR Headset

Color

Do you need somewhere to place your VR headset while also making it stand out? Regardless of your headset, show off your amazing piece of technology to friends, family, and guests! Virtual Reality is such an amazing technology and deserves an elegant and easy way to display for all to see.

Protect your virtual reality headset on a custom colored wall mountable holder to save space on a desk or table, while maintaining easy accessibility for using your headset. This comes in two pieces, the headset wall mount and the controller/touch controller wall mount. Simply place your headset and controllers on the wall stand to store easily store it. With the headset having the lenses and displays hidden underneath the headset, light will not be able to easily enter and cause damage to the displays.

This wall mount can be hung on two level screws or nails on the wall. Choose a from a variety of colors including Black, White, Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow.

All Virtual Reality Headset and Controller Wall Mounts are made to order per customer request. This does not include the headset or controllers.
