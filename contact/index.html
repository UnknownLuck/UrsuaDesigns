VR Headset Stand and Controller Wall Mount

Display Your Prized VR Headset

$24.99

Color

Do you need somewhere to place your VR headset while also making it stand out? Regardless of your headset, show off your amazing piece of technology to friends, family, and guests! Virtual Reality is such an amazing technology and deserves an elegant and easy way to display for all to see.

Protect your virtual reality headset on a custom colored wall mountable holder to save space on a desk or table, while maintaining easy accessibility for using your headset. This comes in two pieces, the headset wall mount and the controller/touch controller wall mount. Simply place your headset and controllers on the wall stand to store easily store it. With the headset having the lenses and displays hidden underneath the headset, light will not be able to easily enter and cause damage to the displays.

This wall mount can be hung on two level screws or nails on the wall. Choose a from a variety of colors including Black, White, Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow.
